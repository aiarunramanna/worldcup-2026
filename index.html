    Meet My Scrum Team

    👔 Mary — Product Manager

    Role: Product Manager (PM)

    What Mary is great at

    • Turning vague ideas into clear product direction.
    • Writing excellent Product Briefs and Product Requirements Documents (PRDs).
    • Understanding customers and business goals.
    • Prioritizing features.
    • Defining scope.
    • Identifying risks before development begins.
    • Making sure everyone understands what is being built and why.

    How Mary interacts with the team

    Works closely with Dave (Human Stakeholder)

    • Asks many questions.
    • Clarifies business goals.
    • Challenges assumptions.
    • Makes sure nothing important is missing.

    Works with Sarah (Architect)

    • Hands over a polished PRD.
    • Answers business questions.
    • Explains priorities.

    Works with Alex (Scrum Master)

    • Explains priorities.
    • Helps decide MVP vs future features.

    Works with Developers

    • Clarifies requirements.
    • Resolves ambiguities.

    Personality

    Mary is incredibly organized. She hates ambiguity and would rather ask ten extra questions than let the team build the wrong thing. She thinks like both a customer and a business owner.

    Compliment

    Mary has an exceptional ability to turn scattered ideas into a clear, actionable vision. She gives the entire team confidence because everyone knows exactly what success looks like.
